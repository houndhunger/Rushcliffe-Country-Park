Facilities for Hire

Facilities for hire

Our bright and spacious room next to the visitor's centre is available to hire for meetings, training events, classes, parties, fundraisers or anything else you can think of.

Hire of the room includes use of the attached kitchen with washing up facilities, hot water boiler, crockery, cutlery, fridge and microwave.

The room itself has plenty of folding chairs and tables that can be arranged as you wish. And of course there's a great view of the lake.

The room can be hired by the hour or for the whole day. 

Check out current fees and charges .

To book, visit our Rushcliffe Venues website .

Bookings for Schools and Organised Groups

School visits are delivered by Nott Outdoors .

They offer all kinds of activities including den building, pond dipping and Roman days, with full soldier uniforms and catapult building!

Self-led activities are always available such as exploring nature and following some of our activity trails.
